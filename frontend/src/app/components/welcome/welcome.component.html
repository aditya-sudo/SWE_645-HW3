Computer Science Department

The Department of Computer Science at George Mason University is a vibrant and dynamic academic unit known for offering a comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs in computer science. Located in Fairfax, Virginia, GMU's computer science department benefits from its proximity to the nation's capital, providing students with valuable opportunities for internships and career prospects in the technology sector and government agencies. The department is distinguished by its accomplished faculty, cutting-edge research initiatives spanning various areas of computer science, and state-of-the-art facilities and resources. Additionally, students can engage in student organizations and clubs related to computer science, fostering a collaborative and innovative learning environment.

George Mason CS Department offers the following courses:

  • Master of Science in Computer Science (CS)
  • Master of Science in Software Engineering (SWE)
  • Master of Science in Information Systems (INFS)
  • Master of Science in Information Security and Assurance (ISA)
